Slam Dunk

Slam Dunk is a sports-themed manga series written and illustrated by Takehiko Inoue about a basketball team from Shohoku High School.It was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jumo from 1990 to 1996,with the chapters collected into 31 tankobon volumes by Shueisha.It was adapted into an anime series by Toei Animation which has been broadcast worldwide,enjoying much popularity particularly in Japan, several other Asian countries and Europe.As of 2012,Slam Dunk had sold 120 million copies in Japan alone,making it one of the best-selling ,anga series in history.Inoue later used basketball as a central theme in two subsequent manga titles:Buzzer Beater and Real.In 2010,Inoue received special commendations from the Japan Basketball Association for helping pupularize basketball in Japan.
